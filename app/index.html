About

Based in Sacramento, California, Restoration Photography aims to bring your historic photos back to life. By repairing rips, tears, stains, discolorations, and countless other plagues of time, Restoration Photography brings family history into the 21st century.

For over twenty years, our restoration experts have been revitalizing photographic relics using the latest technology. By mirroring other elements of the photograph, our artists can recreate a complete picture. When sensitivity of the image requires, this process can also be completed by hand, invigorating treasures of the past with modern craftsmanship.


Highlights

Some of our finest work comes from cases in which the owner thought all hope was lost. A childhood photograph, the only remaining left from a house-fire, charred in half. A neglected wedding portrait, left to rot in a garage for a century. This is the work that infuses our artistry with purpose and inspiration - the possiblity of bringing a never before seen image to your family.


Process
Digitizing

Once a photo is received, we begin the process of high-resolution scanning. After a digital image has been created, we begin analysis of the photograph's integrity, the condition of the image, weak points of resolution, or blur.

Communicating

After assessing the damage to the photograph, our artists will reach out directly to understand your goals and needs. We have many options available, from basic reprinting, to restoration or hand-coloring of antique photographs. Through consultation, we'll provide a quote on services requested.

Restorating

An expert in photographic technology will work to bring your vision to life, filling in tears in an image, erasing dust and pollution on the original photograph, and recoloring fading or destruction. The photograph can also be altered to highlight essential components, by erasing cluttered backgrounds, or using two images to create a composite image with the best of both pieces.

Printing

Your image will be printed on high-quality paper or canvas, depending on your preference. Prints include the finest paper available, to protect against future wear and tear. For an additional fee, the digital image itself can be purchased to allow for at-home printing as well.

Framing

Our artists are happy to select an appropriate frame to further protect your revitalized photograph. We offer custom matting and framing to accentuate your restored piece of history.
